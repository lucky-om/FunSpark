⚡ Rules & Regulations ⚡

Tournament Rules & Regulations

  • Open to participants aged 12+ years.
  • Players under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.
  • FunSpark employees may participate only with management approval and receive no special consideration.
  • 4 players per team — no more, no less.
  • No player substitutions after registration is complete.
  • A player may represent only one team throughout the tournament.
  • Entry Fee: ₹1,000 per team.
  • Registration is confirmed only after full payment.
  • Fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Teams will play a qualifying round as scheduled by FunSpark.
  • Team score = combined score of all 4 players.
  • Top-scoring teams qualify for the Grand Finale.
  • FunSpark may modify the format based on the number of registrations.
  • Teams may attempt a re-entry to improve their score.
  • Fee: ₹1,000 per team per attempt.
  • Highest score achieved across all attempts will be considered.
  • Subject to lane availability and schedule.
  • Report 30 minutes before your scheduled slot.
  • Late arrivals may face disqualification or game forfeiture.
  • Respect fellow players, staff, and officials at all times.
  • Misconduct, cheating, abusive behavior, or property damage may result in immediate disqualification without refund.
  • FunSpark bowling balls and shoes are provided and available.
  • Personal equipment is allowed only with official approval.
  • System-generated scores are final and cannot be disputed.

If teams are tied, the following order applies:

  1. Higher individual game score wins.
  2. If still tied, a one-frame roll-off will be conducted.
  3. Officials' decision will be final in all cases.

Updates, scores, fixtures, and leaderboards will be shared via:

  • 🌐 Website — funsparksurat.in
  • 📸 Instagram — @funspark_surat
  • 💬 WhatsApp

Participants are responsible for staying updated.

  • Photos and videos may be taken during the event.
  • FunSpark may use event media for promotional purposes without additional consent or compensation.
  • Winners must provide valid ID to claim prizes.
  • Prizes are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash unless stated otherwise.
  • Tournament committee and management decisions are final.
  • FunSpark may amend rules, schedules, or tournament structure for operational reasons.
  • Participation is at the player's own risk.
  • FunSpark is not responsible for any injury, loss, theft, or damage to personal belongings.
🎳 By registering, all participants agree to abide by these Rules & Regulations.
